Consider $v\in M_n(\mathbb{R})$. Then $\gamma(t)=I+tv$ is a curve in $GL_n(\mathbb{R})$, for $t$ small, with $\gamma(0)=I$. Then $\dot\gamma(0)=v$. It follows that

$$ \begin{aligned} T_IF(v)=(F\circ\gamma)^\boldsymbol{\cdot}(0)&=\frac{d}{dt}\bigg|_0(I+tv)^\mathsf{T}(I+tv) \newline &=\frac{d}{dt}\bigg|_0(I+t(v^\mathsf{T}+v)+t^2v^\mathsf{T}v) \newline &=v^\mathsf{T}+v. \end{aligned} $$

Since $\operatorname{Sym}^2(\mathbb{R}^n)$ is a vector space, its tangent space can be identified with itself. For every $B\in\operatorname{Sym}^2(\mathbb{R}^n)$, one can write $$ B=\frac{1}{2}(B^\mathsf{T}+B). $$ Hence, $T_IF$ is onto.
